The Morris Worm

Robert Tappan Morris created the first internet computer worm, causing $10 million to $100 million in damage and infecting roughly 6,000 to 60,000 computers (about 10% of the internet at the time).

CIH Virus (1998)

Also known as "Chernobyl," this virus infected 60 million computers internationally, accelerating the need for commercial antivirus software.
